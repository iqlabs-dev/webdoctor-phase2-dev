Trust & Verification Policy
How trust and verification are handled when using iQWEB.

iQWEB is a product operated by iQLABS Ltd (New Zealand). This policy explains how we handle report integrity, security, privacy, verification, and governance when you access or use iQWEB.

Report IDs

Every iQWEB report includes a unique, non-guessable Report ID. This confirms the report was generated by iQWEB and provides a stable reference that can be shared with clients, stakeholders, or teams.

From 2026 onwards, you’ll be able to paste a Report ID into this page to verify:

  • The report is genuine
  • The data matches the original scan
  • The timestamp has not been altered
How Report IDs work:
• A cryptographic hash is created from scan metadata.
• No page content, cookies, or user data are included.
• The ID proves authenticity — nothing more.
What we store

iQWEB stores only the data needed to show your scan history inside your dashboard. We do not store full page content or scrape websites beyond the URL you provide.

Security & privacy

iQWEB is built as a health check — not a data farm. We don’t store, reuse, resell, or train models on your scan data. Processing is handled through the Λ i Q™ Core Engine under New Zealand jurisdiction.

That means:

  • Clear governance and privacy law (New Zealand)
  • No overseas scraping or sharing
  • No third-party injections or cookies added to scanned sites
  • No client tracking, fingerprinting, or behavioural analytics
  • Temporary scan cache is wiped immediately after report generation
Technical notes:
• HTTPS enforced end-to-end
• No scripts injected into scanned sites
• Full server-side processing via Λ i Q™
• No persistent storage of page content
• Dashboard history is optional — delete any scan any time
What we never do
  • Track your users
  • Inject cookies into your site
  • Analyse beyond the public URL you provide
  • Reuse your reports or scan content
  • Hide “AI training” on your data
Trust roadmap

To strengthen verification further, we’re building:

  • A public Report-ID verification API
  • Cryptographic report signatures
  • A transparency log for every engine update
  • A public uptime & reliability dashboard
  • Trust badges for agencies and white-label users
