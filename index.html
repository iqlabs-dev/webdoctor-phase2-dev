The nine signals every iQWEB scan checks

Performance

How fast your pages load and respond for real users.

  • Actual load behaviour and responsiveness
  • Asset weight and request efficiency
  • Server response and TTFB patterns

SEO Foundations

How clearly search engines can understand and rank your site.

  • Title, meta and canonical clarity
  • Indexability and crawl readiness
  • Basic schema and search signals

Structure & Semantics

Whether your HTML scaffolding communicates meaning.

  • Heading hierarchy
  • Core landmarks (header, main, nav, footer)
  • Missing or broken structural elements

Mobile Experience

How your site behaves on the screens your customers actually use.

  • Responsive layout integrity
  • Tap target sizing and spacing
  • Readability on small devices

Security & Trust

Signals that protect users and reinforce credibility.

  • Protocol and redirect behaviour
  • Key security headers (HSTS, CSP, X-Frame-Options, etc.)
  • Mixed-content and downgrade risks

Accessibility

How usable your site is for people using assistive technologies.

  • Contrast, labels and ARIA usage
  • Alt text coverage for key imagery
  • Common keyboard / screen reader traps

Domain & Hosting Health

Behind-the-scenes signals that determine reliability.

  • DNS, SPF and DMARC presence
  • SSL validity and expiry window
  • Server response and platform fingerprints

Content Signals

Whether your pages send clear, strong relevance signals.

  • Title & description length sanity checks
  • Thin or near-empty pages
  • Social preview / Open Graph hygiene

Summary & Fix Plan

A client-ready action plan you can hand to your team or client.

  • Executive summary in human language
  • Ranked ‚ÄúTop Issues‚Äù list
  • Step-by-step Recommended Fix Sequence
