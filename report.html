Recommended Fix Sequence

Phase 1 — Fast wins

Today / This week
  • Address the clearest, highest-leverage issues surfaced in the evidence blocks.
  • Fix metadata/semantics gaps that are straightforward and measurable.
  • Re-run the scan to confirm the change is reflected in signals.

Phase 2 — Structural improvements

1–3 weeks
  • Stabilise performance bottlenecks that require engineering changes.
  • Improve structure and semantics to support SEO and accessibility together.
  • Reduce recurring sources of layout/CLS risk where applicable.

Phase 3 — Hardening & trust

Ongoing
  • Strengthen security posture using modern headers and best practices where appropriate.
  • Implement monitoring and keep regression risk low over time.
  • Schedule periodic accessibility checks as part of ongoing maintenance.
This sequence is designed to be practical: measurable wins first, structural improvements second, long-term hardening last.
