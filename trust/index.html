iQWEB Trust & Verification

Your reports should be reliable, verifiable, and tamper-proof. This page explains how iQWEB handles report integrity, security, privacy, verification, and governance — in plain language.

Report IDs

Every iQWEB report includes a unique, non-guessable Report ID. This confirms the report was generated by iQWEB and hasn’t been altered after export.

From 2026 onwards, you’ll be able to paste any Report ID into this page to verify:

  • The report is genuine
  • The data matches the original scan
  • The timestamp hasn’t been altered
How Report IDs Work

• A cryptographic hash is created from the scan metadata.
• No page content, cookies, or user data are included.
• The ID proves authenticity — nothing more.

Security & Privacy

Plain-language security overview.

iQWEB is built as a health check — not a data farm. We don’t store, reuse, resell, or train models on your scan data.
All processing is handled through the Λ i Q™ Core Engine under New Zealand jurisdiction.

That means:

  • Clear New Zealand governance and privacy law
  • No overseas scraping or sharing
  • No third-party injections or cookies
  • No client tracking, fingerprinting, or behavioural analytics
  • Temporary scan cache is wiped immediately after report generation
Technical Notes

• HTTPS enforced end-to-end
• No scripts injected into scanned sites
• Full server-side processing via Λ i Q™
• No persistent storage of page content
• Dashboard history is optional — delete any scan at any time

What We Never Do

  • No tracking your users
  • No cookies injected into your site
  • No analysis beyond the public URL you provide
  • No reuse of your reports or scan content
  • No hidden “AI training” on your data

  • A public uptime & reliability dashboard
  • Trust badges for agencies and white-label users
